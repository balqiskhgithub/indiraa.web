General Info!

ATEEZ is a K-Pop group formed by KQ Entertainment . Before debut, they had the name KQ FELLAZ. Taken from the name of their agency. Only on July 3 2018, KQ FELLAZ finally officially changed its name to ATEEZ.

Their name is an acronym for "A TEEnager Z", It also stands for "A to Z" meaning they are a group of teenagers who are aiming to do everything they can enthusiastically.

ATINY is ATEEZ's official fandom name. The name was announced by ATEEZ on V Live on November 17, 2018. The acronym ATINY stands for "ATEEZ and destiny", and it denotes that ATEEZ's future is inextricably linked to their fans.

They debuted on October 24, 2018 with the extended play (EP) Treasure EP.1: All to Zero. Ateez released their debut, alongside music videos for the two lead singles "Pirate King" and "Treasure".

Most who followed KQ Fellaz know that there was a trainee named Lee Junyoung (rapper) who KQ planned on making as the 9th member of Ateez. They ended up debuting with the current 8 members instead.
